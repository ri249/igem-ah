Heli Viskari
Heli, University teacher from the Department of Bioproducts and Biosystems at the Aalto University of Chemical Engineering, is our principal advisor. She arranged some of our initial funding and has been guiding us in the ideation and helping with any practical issues in the lab.
Minna Poranen
Minna is an University Lecturer, Docent in Molecular virology and the first ever advisor that the Aalto-Helsinki team has had from the University of Helsinki. Minna has been a guiding us in the ideation.
Markus Linder
Markus is a Professor of Biomolecular materials from Department of Bioproducts and Biosystems at the Aalto University School of Chemical Engineering and one of our advisors. He has guided and helped us finding experts who would be able to help with our project
Merja Penttilä
Merja is an adjunct professor from the Department of Bioproducts and Biosystems at the Aalto University of Chemical Engineering and Research Professor in Industrial Biotechnology and Synthetic biology at VTT, and the Principal scientist of Synbio Powerhouse. Merja is perhaps the most adept scientist in synthetic biology in Finland and has helped us a lot with guidance, contacts and opportunities such as attending the Biotech theme week.
Christopher Jonkergouw
Chris is a doctoral candidate in Biomolecular materials research group in the Department of Bioproducts and Biosystems. Chris has helped our project starting from lab planning, execution, creation of plasmids constructs – and providing us with some of his plasmids to DNA orders and many practical things on how to navigate through the university’s bureaucracy.
Bartosz Gabryelczyk
Bartosz is an academy postdoctoral researcher working in Biomolecular materials research group, in the Department of Bioproducts and Biosystems at Aalto University. Bartosz has helped us with his expertise and experiences throughout the scope of our project, e.g. with DNA orders, sequencing, protein expression and cell fractioning.
Pekka Heino
Docent, University Lecturer in Molecular and Integrative Biosciences Research Programme at the University of Helsinki, Pekka has helped us in the ideation and planning phase of the project.
Ritva Virkola
Benita Westerlund-Wikström
Eeva Vakkari

Siina Vasama, Jutta Kujasalo, Rauno Bergman and
Rosaliina Munnukka from Heureka Science Center
We got the opportunity to host workshops in Heureka Science Center for an entire day. Siina, Jutta, Rauno, and Rosaliina kindly helped us planning the workshops at an early stage. Siina and Rauno further helped us developing our plans and obtaining the materials for our workshops, as well as setting up and organizing everything on the event day.
Merja Penttilä, Pauli Kallio, Lauri Reuter and Mikko Myllykoski
These professionals from different fields helped us with creating our podcast series about synthetic biology. Each of them took part in our podcast as guest speaker.
