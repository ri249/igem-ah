HUMAN PRACTICES

The two aims of our human practices were integrating stakeholder input to our project design and raising awareness about synthetic biology through educational and engaging activities.

With our integrated human practices, we used a stakeholder-based approach to guide us on developing the goals of our project. This was done to find out how our solution would fit into the real world an toverify, that there is a relevant need for our solution. Find out more.

With our education and public engagement activities, we aimed at including a variety of target groups as possible – from academic professionals to young children – to learn and hear all about science and synthetic biology in fun and engaging ways. As well as grow the synthetic biology community in Finland. Find out more.
